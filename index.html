About Rohit

The Man Who
Runs the Show

Rohit Nailwal is not just a music teacher — he is the driving force behind The Blues Music Classes, Haldwani's most vibrant music learning destination. As founder and Managing Director, Rohit has built a platform that has helped students aged 12 to 68 discover, develop, and fall in love with music.

Educated under the globally respected ABRSM (Associated Board of the Royal Schools of Music) framework and holding a Trinity College London certification, Rohit brings international standards to every session. His teaching philosophy is simple: every student is unique, every lesson should inspire, and music belongs to everyone.

Curriculum

What You Will Learn

A complete musical education — from foundations to performance, every session is purposefully designed.

Music Theory

Understand scales, chords, keys, and harmony — learn to read and write music with real comprehension.

Technique & Posture

Proper hand positioning, fingering, and posture — the physical foundation that unlocks true fluency.

Rhythm & Timing

Develop an iron-solid sense of rhythm through exercises, sight-reading, and structured practice.

Composition

Write your own melodies, harmonies, and arrangements — express your unique musical voice.

Ear Training

Identify intervals, chords, and melodies by ear alone — sharpen your musical intuition session by session.

Performance Skills

Stage presence, memorisation, and interpretation — gain the confidence to truly share your music.
